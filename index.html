For Online Learning

Vowpal Wabbit (VW) is the mature open-source online machine learning library for solutions to a wide variety of problems. VW pushes the frontier of machine learning with techniques for online, active, and interactive learning as well as contextual bandits, extreme multiclass, hashing and learning2search.
